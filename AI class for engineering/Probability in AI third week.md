## Total Probability
	P(Y)= sum{P(Y|X=i)*P(X=i)}
	P(notX|Y)=1-P(X|Y)

## Bayes Rule
	P(A|B)={P(B|A)*P(A)}/{P(B)}
	P(B) above always can be caculated from "total probability" method.
